class SiteCrawler:

View In Hierarchy

A sitecrawler is a generator that yields a tuple of the form (PreParser, file) Its goal is to create a list of preparsers and files to be parsed based on the files and directories in the initial path. This generator is to be used in a for loop to parse all the files and produce the final website.
